Admissions

Your path to Ashcombe.

We admit need-blind and meet 100% of demonstrated financial need. Here's exactly what the process looks like, start to finish.

The Process

Four steps to apply.

01

Submit your application

Common App or Ashcombe's own portal, plus a $75 application fee (waivable).

02

Send transcripts & scores

Official high school transcripts; standardized tests are optional for 2026–27.

03

Interview (optional)

Alumni interviews are offered, not required, and never count against you.

04

Receive your decision

Decisions released via the applicant portal on the dates listed below.

Requirements

What you'll need.

  • ✓

    Completed application form

    Common App, Coalition App, or Ashcombe's direct portal.

  • ✓

    Official transcript

    Sent directly from your school counselor's office.

  • ✓

    Two letters of recommendation

    At least one from a core academic subject teacher.

  • ✓

    Personal essay

    650 words responding to one of the Common App prompts.

  • ✓

    Financial aid forms (if applying for aid)

    FAFSA and CSS Profile, due by the same deadline as your application.
