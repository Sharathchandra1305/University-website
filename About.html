About Ashcombe

Built on 135 years of rigorous inquiry.

Since 1888, Ashcombe University has trained scholars, scientists, and civic leaders — guided by a founding belief that education should be both demanding and deeply human.

Our History

A timeline of growth.

1888

Founding charter granted

Ashcombe opens its doors with a single school of Arts & Sciences and 42 students.

1924

School of Law established

Ashcombe Law becomes one of the first law schools in the region to admit women.

1961

School of Engineering founded

A gift from alumnus Walter Combe funds the university's first engineering labs.

2003

Research expenditures top $100M

Ashcombe enters the top tier of national research institutions.

2026

18,000+ students, 70+ countries

Today, Ashcombe is home to one of the most internationally diverse student bodies in the country.
